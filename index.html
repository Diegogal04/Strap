Our Story

Strap is a newly founded start up, which has made it its business to connect strangers all over the world to make quarantine a bit more bearable. The founding team behind started the project as a hobby, but due to the great demand the hobby soon became a profession for the team from Mexico. After only one month it was clear that Strap could be the next big hit. The Strap Team consists of 4 people, all aged 17 to 18. Although the team behind Strap is very young, the team's self-taught coding skills should not be underestimated. In a short time, the young team was able to write software that allows all people in the world to connect to each other to kill time during quarantine.
